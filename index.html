Rangsaaz

We are a group of like-minded people that have a common interest and passion for Indian handicrafts. We love all things colorful and the wide array of Indian artforms satiate this passion of ours. So we at Rangsaaz are determined to show the world the beauty that lies in these artforms. Understanding the skill, patience, and hard work behind each craft is our motive to truly appreciate the art. And we want to provide this platform to all the craftsmen out there. ​Hop on the bandwagon if you also want to either showcase your craft, pick up a new craft or even just help us spread the word. Come be a part of this colorful journey.
